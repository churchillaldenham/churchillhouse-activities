How the Evening Works

1
You start the evening with a set of chips. These are your currency for the night.
2
Move freely between the game stations and use your chips to play.
3
Each station has a member of staff who will explain the game and run each round. Not sure about anything? Just ask.
4
At the end of the evening, the three students with the most chips win a prize.

Blackjack
Get closer to 21 than the dealer — without going over
Up to 5 players 2–4 min/round
▾
How to Play
1
Place your bet (1–3 chips) before any cards are dealt.
2
You are dealt 2 cards face up. The dealer gets 2 cards — one face up, one face down.
3
Choose: TWIST (take another card) or STICK (stay with what you have).
4
If you go over 21, you BUST and lose your bet immediately.
5
When everyone has finished, the dealer reveals their hidden card.
6
Closest to 21 without busting beats the dealer.
🏆 How to Win
Beat the dealer → win double your bet
Blackjack (Ace + picture card as first 2 cards) → win triple your bet
Tie with dealer → get your bet back
Bust (over 21) → lose your bet
💡 Tip: Ace can be 1 or 11 — your choice. Picture cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10.

Three Card Poker
Get a better 3-card hand than the dealer
4–6 players 2–3 min/round
▾
How to Play
1
Place your bet (1–3 chips) before any cards are dealt.
2
You get 3 cards face down. The dealer gets 3 cards — one is face up so you can see it.
3
Look at your cards. Decide: FOLD (give up your bet and sit out) or PLAY (put the same number of chips again and stay in).
4
The dealer reveals their cards.
5
Compare hands — best hand wins.
🏆 How to Win
Your hand beats the dealer → win equal to your total bet (both stacks)
Dealer beats you → dealer takes your chips
Tie → get your chips back

Higher or Lower
Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower
1 player 30–60 sec/turn
▾
How to Play
1
Place your bet (1–3 chips).
2
Seven cards are placed face down in a row. The first card is turned over.
3
Guess: will the next card be HIGHER or LOWER? The card is revealed.
4
Correct → move to the next card. Wrong → you lose your bet immediately.
5
Keep going until you are wrong or reach the end of all 7 cards.
🏆 How to Win
Get 5 correct → win double your bet
Get all 7 correct → win triple your bet
Wrong at any point → lose your bet
💡 Tip: Ace is the highest card. If the next card is the same value as the current one, it counts as wrong.

Shut the Box
Roll the dice and close the tiles. Lower score wins
1–4 players 2–3 min/player
▾
How to Play
1
Pay 1 chip to play. All tiles (1–9) start open.
2
Roll both dice. Add the two numbers together to get your total.
3
Close one or more tiles that add up to your total. You choose which combination.
4
Example: Roll a 7. You can close: 7 alone / 3+4 / 1+6 / 2+5 / 1+2+4.
5
Keep rolling until no combination of open tiles matches your roll.
6
Your score = the total of all tiles still open. Lower is better.
🏆 How to Win
Score over 10 → lose your chip
Score 10 or under → win 2 chips back
Score 5 or under → win 4 chips back
Score 0 (Shut the Box!) → win 10 chips — JACKPOT! 🎉
💡 Tip: Think carefully about which tiles to close — keeping smaller tiles open gives you more options on future rolls.

Crown and Anchor
Pick a symbol. If the dice land on it, you win
4–6 players Under 1 min/round
▾
How to Play
1
Place 1–3 chips on any symbol on the mat: Crown, Anchor, Heart, Diamond, Club or Spade. You can bet on more than one symbol.
2
The dealer rolls three dice.
3
For every die that shows your symbol, you win that many chips — plus your stake back.
4
No dice show your symbol → you lose your stake.
🏆 How to Win
1 die matches → win 1 chip per chip bet, plus stake back
2 dice match → win 2 chips per chip bet, plus stake back
3 dice match → win 3 chips per chip bet, plus stake back
No match → lose your stake
💡 Example: Bet 2 chips on Crown. Two dice show Crown → win 4 chips plus your 2 back. Total: 6 chips.

Mexico
Roll the highest number. Last player standing wins the pot
4–6 players 5–10 min/game
▾
How to Play
1
Everyone puts 1 chip into the pot.
2
Roll two dice. Read them as a two-digit number — highest digit first. Example: roll 5 and 2 = 52. Roll 6 and 1 = 61.
3
After your roll, you can re-roll both dice once if you want. Your second roll stands — you cannot go back.
4
Once everyone has rolled, the player with the lowest score is eliminated.
5
Everyone still in puts another chip into the pot. Play the next round.
6
Keep going until one player remains — they win the whole pot.
🏆 How to Win
Mexico (2+1 = 21) → beats everything — highest possible score
Doubles beat any non-double of a higher value (e.g. 33 beats 65)
Doubles ranking: 66 > 55 > 44 > 33 > 22 > 11
Lowest score each round → eliminated
Last player remaining → wins the pot
💡 Tip: You don't have to re-roll. If you roll a high number, stick with it.

Spin the Wheel
Predict the wheel. Land on a Dare — and the real fun begins
1 player 1–2 min/turn
▾
How to Play
1
Place your bet (1–3 chips) and call ODD or EVEN before the wheel is spun.
2
The wheel is spun.
3
Odd or Even — if your call matches, you win equal to your bet. Wrong call → lose your bet.
4
Banker — lose 3 chips, no matter what you called.
5
Dare — pick a Dare card. Complete the dare to win triple your bet. Refuse or fail → lose your bet.
🏆 How to Win
Correct call (Odd or Even) → win equal to your bet
Wrong call → lose your bet
Banker → lose 3 chips
Dare — complete it → win triple your bet
Dare — refuse or fail → lose your bet
💡 Tip: Dare challenges are decided by the staff member at the station. Their decision is final — no arguments!

Jenga for Chips
Pull a block. Complete the challenge. Don't knock the tower
2–6 players 10–15 min/game
▾
How to Play
1
Everyone puts 1 chip into the pot at the start.
2
On your turn, pull one block from the tower using one hand only. You cannot touch the top layer. You have 20 seconds.
3
Place the block on top of the tower, then read what is written on it.
4
Challenge block: attempt the challenge in 20 seconds. Success → win double your starting bet from the pot. Refuse or fail → pay 1 chip into the pot.
5
Multiplier block (2x, 3x, 5x): win that multiple of your starting bet from the pot.
6
Lose block: pay 1 chip into the pot.
7
Other blocks: follow the instruction.
🏆 How to Win
Complete a challenge → win double your starting bet from the pot
Hit a 2x, 3x or 5x block → win that multiple of your starting bet
Knock the tower over → pay 3 chips into the pot — and the last successful player wins the pot
Refuse a challenge → pay 1 chip into the pot
💡 Tip: If you hesitate more than 5 seconds before starting a challenge, it counts as a refusal.
